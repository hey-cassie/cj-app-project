About Me

Hey! I’m Cassie! I’m a freelance graphic designer, illustrator, printmaker and wannabe front end web developer,
specializing in logo design, brand identity, UI, and illustration. :)



I’m from sunny Southern California and currently live in St. Louis, MO. My work is largely inspired by my beachy/sandy upbringing; i draw inspiration from the so cal skate and surf culture and am endlessly inspired by visual artists like big daddy roth, bill ogden, rick griffen, mike kelly, jeffery vallance, chris burden, musicians like the descendents, minutemen, the talking heads



I graduated with a MFA in Visual Art from Washington University in St. Louis in 2014, and I also hold a BFA in Printmaking from Cal State Long Beach (2012). I love turning my drawings into screen prints and have my own DIY printing studio set up in my basement where I crank out small editions.



I have a bossy little chihuahua that I freakin’ adore. We call him Lemmy and, in our household, Lemmy is God. I also ride bikes! When I'm not working on a creative project, noodling with code, or cuddling with Lemmy I’m usually out shredding on my single speed Nature Boi.
